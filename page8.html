HOW IT WORKS OUR STRATEGY

Responsible 100's aim is to make real responsibility the profit-maximising option and reveal our path to a sustainable, fair and livable future.

While clear on the need for fundamental reform, and certain of their desire to help build and be part of an economy that serves the interests of all people and planet, business leaders often admit that they don’t know what to change or how.

Responsible 100’s purpose is to recruit and support those leaders and their businesses to identify and enact the necessary changes.

So that, together, we can calculate what changes are required to create an ideal version of capitalism, and then catalyse the transition to it. That is, a version of capitalism that functions in the service of people and planet, one that generates shared health, wealth and happiness.

TAKE RESPONSIBILITY,
RESCUE PROSPERITY

We have a choice. Two paths lay ahead of us. One is perilous, the other full of promise. One leads us to climate change, and eventual ecological and societal collapse. The other requires systemic change, and leads us to a healthy, prosperous future. 

It is a simple choice: change or be changed.  The first path – be changed – is the path we are on today. The ideas and assumptions that have given us so much prosperity to date are failing us.

If we continue with them, if we don’t take responsibility for their failure, our prosperity will not last. If we want a prosperous future for ourselves, our families and our descendents, then we need to choose to change, now.

Choices like this are rare. The chance to shift and accelerate the direction of human progress, to rescue us from the jaws of failure, to define a new era of prosperity for all. That is a once in a lifetime opportunity.

We need to take responsibility for this choice. Choosing to change means choosing to act responsibly.

To choose the path of promise, of profit, of prosperity, we must be responsible. We know we must act. We know we must uncover exactly what changes to make and how to make them to create and be part of the future we know in our hearts is possible. 

We know we can do it.
