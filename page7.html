Why Responsible 100? And why now?

We're helping all types of organisations, on all critical responsibility issues, with a systems-changing tool

All businesses face these fundamental questions:

1

Which responsibility issues matter most to my company?

2

Which responsibility issues matter most to civil society, and to our stakeholders?

3

How is my company doing on those issues? Where could we or should we improve?

4

How and when can we make those improvements? How can we measure and monitor our progress over time (and report internally or externally)?

"Responsible 100 has a compelling offer to businesses seeking to better understand the most important social and environmental issues, and what practices to stop, add, or modify, in order to improve performance. The ultimate test will be delivering their services profitability and at scale, and I'll bet on them achieving success and having extraordinary impact on the world."
Head of Sustainability EMEA and Partner, KPMG

How it works

Year on year, the case for pursuing the most ambitious version of our business tool cum social movement has increased. Now is the time to combine what we've learnt and created into complete, paid-for service offerings for businesses. And by levering and matching the needs and resources of our supporters in so doing.

This is our strategy. In essence it is to prove real responsibility can be a driver of profitability by making our services commercially compelling to all. Irrespective of size or sector, or starting point, we can help any business answer the universally urgent questions set out above. We do so efficiently, effectively and affordably.

We create increasing reward for those businesses posing these questions and acting on the answers. In turn, this creates critical economic incentives for others to follow suit. We’re improving our processes all the time and profitably delivering services of demonstrable value. We’re also exploring the best, most assured opportunities to scale up.
