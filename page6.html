OUR PURPOSE

Making real responsibility the profit-maximising option to reveal our path to a sustainable, fair and livable future

While clear on the need for fundamental reform, and certain of their desire to help build and be part of a fair, sustainable, livable economy, business leaders often admit that they don’t know what to change now or how best to do it.

Responsible 100’s purpose is to recruit and support those leaders and their businesses to identify and enact the necessary changes.

So that, together, we can calculate what changes are required to create an ideal version of capitalism, and then catalyse the transition to it.

That is, a version of capitalism that functions in the service of people and planet, one that generates shared health, wealth and happiness.

A version that enables everyone to thrive within and between their communities, in harmony with nature, and within the Earth's planetary limits.

Responsible 100 is at the cutting edge of efforts to #buildbackbetter
