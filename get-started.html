Partner With Us Simple Steps
YOUR SIMPLE STEPS
The value you will derive from participation, and the value you will create for the Responsible 100 community, is directly related to how many steps you take, and how many of the 22 issues you explore and assess against.
1. PRIORITISE

PRIORITISE

We currently look at 22 responsibility issues. They can't all be CRITICAL. So, firstly, indicate the priority level of each issue as you see it for your company. 

This is something one person can do, or something the whole team can be involved in. 

2. EXPLORE

EXPLORE THE BENCHMARKS

We've broken down all the issues we explore into bite sized pieces.

Select the examples of policies and practices that describe what you do, and indicate some of your reasons. In minutes, we'll help you paint an accurate picture of what happens in your business. 

3. SAY IT SIMPLY

SAY IT SIMPLY 

We use AI to create draft accounts of what you do based on the selections you make in Step 2.

It takes only a matter of minutes to do this and to thus get you started.

But ultimately, you must be satisfied that your responses are complete, accurate and verifiable. Your account of what you do, and your future plans, must be your own accounts in your words. 

4. SCORE

SCORE YOURSELF

So, after all that, how well do you perform? Really?

We use the simplest scale. On each issue we explore we identify 4 performance levels: POOR, OKAY, GOOD and EXCELLENT.

In the event that we don't agree what level of performance you've achieved, no drama, we simply ask our expert partners to decide. 

5. REPEAT

REPEAT 

Your Responsibility Indicator combines all the above, across each of the issues, in one powerful dashboard.

Use it to set plans for future work and improvements and to keep track of progress. Use it to communicate the stories of what you do both internally, or externally.
