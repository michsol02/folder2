HOW IT WORKS

A pro-business, pro-profit and collaborative initiative

 Responsible 100 is a pro-business, pro-profit and collaborative initiative, open to all.

It includes businesses large and small, and from various sectors, as well as a wide range of leading NGOs and think tanks.

Through a managed process within a safe and supportive space, we're charting current business performance on a variety of critical social, environmental and ethical issues.

We’re co-creating the road maps businesses need to navigate their way to perform better on key responsibility issues, and benefit themselves and everyone else in so doing.

We help businesses to determine whether they may be negatively impacting people and planet in respect of these issues. We work out what it will take to cease any such negative impacts in the future, and preferably impact positively instead. And we identify significant, actionable opportunities for improvement.

With an ever clearer view of current performance, and of ideal future performance, we can determine the changes to policy and practice that are required to get businesses onto an increasingly sustainable trajectory. And we’re also able to detect the drivers and barriers to the adoption of those changes, and the costs of action and inaction.

We're helping individual businesses to identify and implement the changes with the greatest impact. We help uncover the best opportunities to act, and how to do that, which fit their circumstances and can be adopted at their level.

Responsible 100 harnesses the power of the collective. Together, we’re able to pool and share knowledge, experience and know-how. We can describe the various challenges and barriers we’ve encountered, and which we’ve been able to overcome and how. Together, we can best innovate for the common good.

Responsible 100 supports and influences positive change across sectors. Through open processes and the clever use and application of data, and by aligning the interests of businesses large and small with those of a wide range of NGOs, campaign groups, academics, unions and trade bodies, think tanks and governmental bodies, we’re weaponising real responsibility as a driver of business success and profitability.

We’re improving the Responsible 100 tools and processes all the time and making it ever easier for businesses and supporters to join, contribute and benefit. 

Simple first steps...

Step 1

Complete your issues ranking assessement - for free - using our simple questionnaire. We'll provide a visualisation of your results and commentary the next working day.

Step 2

Get expert guidance from the R100 team - online, over the telephone, or in person - to identify your opportunities for improvement and determine your focus areas.

Step 3

Together we'll draw up a Top 10 or Top 20 list of priority issues to focus and work on. We'll draw up a bespoke and actionable plan, and guide and support you to improve.
