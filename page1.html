HOW IT WORKS

Working out the changes needed to help create and be part of an economy that serves the best interests of all people and the planet

 Responsible 100 manages an innovative process within a safe and supportive space to chart current business performance on a variety of critical social, environmental and ethical issues.

We help businesses to determine whether they may be negatively impacting people and planet in respect of these issues. We work out what it will take to cease any such negative impacts in the future, and preferably impact positively instead. And we identify significant, actionable opportunities to make improvements.

Through open processes and the clever use and application of data, we’re co-creating the road maps businesses need to navigate their way to perform better on key responsibility issues, and benefit themselves and everyone else in so doing.

With an ever clearer view of current performance, and of ideal future performance, we can determine the changes required to get businesses onto an increasingly sustainable trajectory. And we’re also able to detect the drivers and barriers to making those policy and practice changes, as well as the costs of action and inaction.

Responsible 100 is a powerful collective including businesses large and small, and from various sectors, as well as a wide range of leading NGOs and think tanks. These organisations are united in their desire to help build and participate in a better economy.

Using shared knowledge, experience and know-how we help individual businesses to identify and implement the changes with the greatest impact.

We help uncover the best opportunities to act, and how to do that, which fit their circumstances and can be adopted at their level.
