CORPORATIONS

We have a choice.

Two paths lay ahead of us. One is perilous, the other full of promise.
One leads us to climate change, and eventual ecological and societal collapse.
The other requires systemic change, and leads us to a healthy, prosperous future.

It is a simple choice: change or be changed. 

The first path – be changed – is the path we are on today. The ideas and assumptions that have given us so much prosperity to date are failing us. If we continue with them, if we don’t take responsibility for their failure, our prosperity will not last. If we want a prosperous future for ourselves, our families and our descendents, then we need to choose to change, now.

Choices like this are rare. The chance to shift and accelerate the direction of human progress, to rescue us from the jaws of failure, to define a new era of prosperity for all. That is a once in a lifetime opportunity.

We need to take responsibility for this choice. Choosing to change means choosing to act responsibly.

To choose the path of promise, of profit, of prosperity, we must be responsible. We know we must act. We know we must uncover exactly what changes to make and how to make them to create and be part of the future we know in our hearts is possible.

We know we can do it.

Responsible 100's Purpose

While clear on the need for fundamental reform, and certain of their desire to help build and be part of a fair, sustainable, livable economy, business leaders often admit that they don’t know what to change now or how best to do it.

Responsible 100’s purpose is to recruit and support those leaders and their businesses to identify and enact the necessary changes. Together, we’re calculating what changes are required to create an ideal version of capitalism and catalyse the transition to it.

That is, a version of capitalism that functions in the service of people and planet, one that generates shared health, wealth and happiness. A version that enables everyone to thrive within and between their communities, in harmony with nature, and within the Earth's planetary limits.

HOW IT WORKS

Responsible 100 is a pro-business, pro-profit and collaborative initiative, open to all. It includes businesses large and small, and from various sectors, as well as a wide range of leading NGOs and think tanks.

Through a managed process within a safe and supportive space, we're charting current business performance on a variety of critical social, environmental and ethical issues.
