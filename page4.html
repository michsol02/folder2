Serving SMEs

Understanding and addressing a constantly evolving set of pressing social, environmental and ethical issues is a complex and often overwhelming undertaking for any company. Responsible 100 helps you to better understand which responsibility challenges to focus on, how well you perform, and what you can do to improve.

1

Which Issues Matter Most to My Company? 

Determine the relevance and importance of a wide range of social, environmental and ethical issues. We offer a short, confidential and no-obligation survey, and associated analysis and personalised report. Take it now.

2

How is My Company Doing?

Together, we'll work out how well your business measures up on the issues that matter most, how you compare to your peers, and where improvements can be made.

3

How Can We Improve?

Once you know your priorities, goals can be set, and the necessary steps to make the greatest positive impact can be taken. We'll work out a tailored improvement plan with clear, achievable milestones. 

4

Best Practice Guidelines

Responsible 100 benchmarks map SME performance on over 50 issues across eight broad responsibility categories: Community, Environment, Ethical Choices, Finance and Governance, Human Rights, Marketplace Ethics, Technology, and Workforce. The R100 scorecard library constitutes an incredibly valuable resource. 

5

Consultancy

Having diagnosed problems and areas of underperformance, and the appropriate solutions, we'll help you to plan and deliver the required changes, and support you on the journey as and where required.

6

Reporting & Communicating

We help you to effectively communicate the things you are doing well, and the areas on which you are improving.

7

Differentiation

Responsible 100 is for businesses that want to help create and be part of a sustainable, far, livable future. They are the first choice businesses for the consumers, employees and investors who want that too.

HOW IT WORKS

Responsible 100 is a pro-business, pro-profit and collaborative initiative, open to all. It includes businesses large and small, and from various sectors, as well as a wide range of leading NGOs and think tanks.

Through a managed process within a safe and supportive space, we're charting current business performance on a variety of critical social, environmental and ethical issues.
